The Financial institutions (FIs) are required to electronically submit a TFSA record to us by the last day of February. A TFSA record is similar to a tax slip except that the issuers are not required to send you a paper copy of the record.

This is the latest TFSA information we have on file for you based on the accepted records we received from your FI(s). If changes have been made since this information was originally reported to us, it may not be updated here yet. In addition, the records must be received and accepted by the CRA in order to be reflected on this page. If necessary, you should report any discrepancies to your FI(s).
