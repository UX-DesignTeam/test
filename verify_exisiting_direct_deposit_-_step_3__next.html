For Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) and the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) to share your personal information and direct deposit information, your consent is required.


By selecting "I agree", you agree with these two statements:



• I consent to the CRA sharing with ESDC my direct deposit information used for my income tax refunds, Canada Workers Benefit advance payments, and GST/HST credit payments. The information will be used only to make Canada Pension Plan payments to me, until I tell ESDC to stop using it for my payments.

• I consent to the CRA sharing my social insurance number, last name, and date of birth with ESDC, only so ESDC can identify me correctly.

If you select "I do not agree", you confirm that you do not consent to share your ESDC banking information. For more information on providing your direct deposit banking information to CRA, visit the CRA website.
