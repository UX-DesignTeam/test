If you no longer have the cheque(s), you can request the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) for a duplicate payment by following the instructions below:

Select a payment from the table and click in the link to generate a form “PWGSC 535 UNDERTAKING AND INDEMNITY”.


Print the pre-populated form. The form must be signed by you, as the payee, and must be signed by a witness who is not a family member. Under the signatures, you must include your name and address, as well as the name and address of your witness. Otherwise, the form will be returned to you as not complete.

Note: A legal representative can sign for you, the payee, but the representative must complete the "Authorized Officer" section of the form.


Scan and submit the completed form through the “Submit Docs” feature on My Account or mail it to the following address:

Sudbury Tax Office
